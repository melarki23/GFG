So you have landed yourself a Nerd...Now what?

If you are as lucky as I am you have landed yourself a nerd and if they are anything like mine they have enough hobbies/interests for the both of you! All the nerdiness can be overwhelming at first but through this site I hope to make it a little easier for you to gain a little knowledge on a few subjects that can help you to impress your man. Who knows you may even find a new hobby!

I have enjoyed working on this site, for the second time now! I hope it helps you navigate the journey to all things Nerd!
